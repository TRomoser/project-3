About

Finding affordable events that you want to attend in your city can be difficult enough, and finding convenient, age-inclusive events to attend in your neighborhood can feel next to impossible.

We are a team of three software developers on a mission to create a community-centric app that makes finding events and putting on events simple and fun!




Advantages

Local

All of our events are made by members of the community

Free

Our app will always be free to the users

Connectivity

Build deeper connections with your community over our discussion/comment forums

Safe

As a non-profit, we won't sell your data to a third-party nor bombard you with advertisements
